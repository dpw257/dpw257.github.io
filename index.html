Professional experience and education

Bachelor studies in this field equipped me with strong skills in data analysis, applied mathematics and statistics, while a master's degree in computational linguistics allowed me to explore NLP applications, such as machine translation and sentiment analysis, cumulating in a capstone project in which I applied ML techniques to study grammatical change in a minority language.
My experience in business strategy enables me to bridge the gap between technical solutions and business needs with a solid understanding of product pricing, customer experience and sales techniques.
