Data analysis and statistics


Working at a business consultancy, I was so impressed by the impact I saw data-driven approaches were having in transforming industries and maximising revenue that I decided to switch to a career in data science. Bachelor's studies equipped me with strong skills in data analysis, applied mathematics and statistics, while a master's degree in computational linguistics allowed me to explore NLP applications such as machine translation and sentiment analysis, which cumulated in a capstone project in which I applied machine learning techniques to study linguistic change in a minority language. My experience with business strategy allows me to bridge the gap between technical solutions and business needs, whether it's optimizing product pricing, enhancing customer experience, or analysing sales performance.
